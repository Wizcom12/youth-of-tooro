About Youth Of Tooro sacco

Youth Of Tooro sacco is a community-based savings and credit cooperative society (SACCO) established in (...............) around Rukidi 3 Street , Fort Portal and now with many service centres (SACOO Branches) around Kabarole and Fort Portal sub regions ,with the aim of providing financial services to its members. Over the years, we have grown to become one of the leading SACCOs in the region, serving thousands of members and contributing to the socio-economic development of our community.

At Youth Of Tooro sacco, we are committed to promoting a savings culture and providing affordable financial solutions to our members. Our range of products and services includes savings accounts, loans, mobile banking, and more, all tailored to meet the diverse needs of our members.

With a team of dedicated staff and a strong focus on customer satisfaction, we strive to uphold the values of integrity, transparency, and professionalism in all our operations. Our goal is to empower our members financially and contribute to their overall well-being and prosperity.

General Savings Requirements

  • LC1 Introductory Letter
  • Registration Fee: UGX 77,000
  • 3 Passport Size Photos
  • National ID

General Loan Requirements

  • Membership Fees: UGX 77,000
  • Loan Application Fee: UGX 10,000
  • Photocopy of National/Citizen Identity Card
  • 4 Passport Photos
  • Security (Collateral)
  • Two Guarantors
  • Savings Deposit: At least 5% of the loan amount
  • Shares: At least 10% of the loan amount
  • CRB Report (Credit Reference Bureau)
  • Minimum loan is UGX 100,000; maximum loan is UGX 30,000,000

Location: P.O. Box 100, Rukidi 3 Street , Fort Portal
